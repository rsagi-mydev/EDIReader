About EDI Reader

EDI Reader is a fully client-side tool — your data never leaves your browser. It transforms EDI documents (X12 and EDIFACT) into JSON, XML, or human-readable business PDFs.

Supported transactions

  • X12 810 · Invoice
  • X12 850 · Purchase Order
  • X12 855 · PO Ack
  • X12 856 · Ship Notice
  • X12 940 · Warehouse Ship
  • X12 945 · Warehouse Confirmation
  • X12 997 · Functional Ack
  • EDIFACT INVOIC · ORDERS · DESADV
